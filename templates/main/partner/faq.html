When I get paid?

We pay you every 45 days if your earnings are $100 or more. If your earnings are less than $100, we'll wait until they are $100 and then pay you 45 days after that.


How much do you pay?

You start with 10% commission out of every referal's in-game payments. If you cross $1000, we raise your commission rate to 15% and to 20% if you collect $5000 or more during a month.


For how long my referal is active?

He have no time limit on referals, so you keep receiving your commission as long as refered players are active Car Battle players!
