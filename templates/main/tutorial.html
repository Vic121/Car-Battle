Battles

Is the most fun way of acquiring money and experiend, that let you have new cars.

  1. Select an opponent from lists and click FIGHT
  2. Now you see a battle screen that contains two cars. One on the left hand side is a randomly selected car of your garage, and on the right hand side, your opponent's car. As you see, the ladder has hidden name, photo and attributes at first.
  3. Click on opponent's attribute that you consider worse than yours. Select 3 attributes out of 6 and see if you'll win.
  4. Each fight consists of 3 battles, win at least 2 of them to win whole fight and get a lot of experience and cash.

   

Jobs

As you click on JOBS button, you'll see list of cars. One of then can be yours, Tier 1 car is even already unlocked so you can grab it right away. But it only takes a while to unlock betters ones.

You can either win a battle to unlock next tier, or ask given number of friends to click on a link that is provided in the box. As more people click and register, better cars become unlocked.

Each day 3 jobs can be made, you'll see how much time left to reset on the screen.



Auctions

It's like eBay, but stripped away features to let you start buying and selling easily. Just find a car you need or want to own and bid out your competitors.

Auctions can genearate income of course as car can be sold out of your garage.


   

Albums

So you already have some cars? Take a look at Albums. Albums are manually selected cars based on given subjects, ie. 1980-2000 BMWs, History of Ferrari, etc.

If you have a car listed in an album, in your garage, you can "stick" it to this album. Keep in mind that car once sticked to album is being removed from garage permanenty. But as albums can be seen on your public profile page, everyone watching it can admire your extensive collection. It's also a good way to learn about different manufacturers and models.

Only few albums are available at the beginning, most of them you'll unlock over time.
