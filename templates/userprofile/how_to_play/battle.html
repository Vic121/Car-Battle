• Prove you have the best cars and gain money and experience by battling other players.
  • How it works?
    • choose a opponent; you get extra bonus for higher-than-you level and get penaltied by aiming on weaker ones.
    • you start a three-car battle which means 3 cars randomly selected from your and your opponent's garage.
    • in each battle you choose any three of six car attributes.
    • depending on those attributes you win or lose the skirmish and winner of three wins the whole battle.
